Mango Margarita

Description

This mango margarita recipe is easy to make

with mango nectar for a refreshing, fruity cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice
  • 12 fluid ounces margarita mix
  • 6 fluid ounces tequila
  • 4 fluid ounces mango nectar
  • 1 lime, halved
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or as needed

Steps

  1. Combine ice, margarita mix, tequila, and mango nectar in a blender;

    squeeze 1 lime half into the blender. Blend until cocktail is smooth.

  2. Cut remaining lime half into wedges.

    Pour 1/4 to 1/2 inch of sugar onto a small, shallow plate.

    Moisten rims of 4 glasses with a wedge of lime, dip the moistened glasses into sugar.

    Pour cocktail into the prepared glasses.
